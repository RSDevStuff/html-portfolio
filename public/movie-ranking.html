My Top Movies for a Marathon Watch

You only need three movies, ever.

Ocean's 11

Obviously the original is the best. This movie exudes charm, wit, and has an excellent soundtrack to go along with it's amazing plot.

Ocean's 12

This time France is involved. The subplot with Rusty's girlfriend is cute and all, but felt off. Still a great watch.

Ocean's 13

A great conclusion to the series. Linus gets his wings, the stakes are raised even higher, and there's plenty of obstacles to overcome.
